A mystical arborio rice dish infused with lavender and

Dragon's Breath Noodles

$1

Hand-pulled obsidian noodles made with squid ink, tossed in a
fiery sauce of fermented dragon fruit and Carolina Reaper peppers.
Garnished with crystallized ginger flowers and served in a bowl
that literally steams and smokes. The heat builds slowly, then
erupts like a volcano of flavor.

Levitating Salmon

$1

Wild Atlantic salmon that appears to float above your plate using
hidden magnetic levitation. The fish is cured in Nordic sea salt
and cold-smoked with driftwood. Accompanied by spherified lemon
caviar that bursts on your tongue and a cloud of rosemary smoke
that dissipates to reveal the floating masterpiece.
